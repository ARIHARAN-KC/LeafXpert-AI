Leaf Disease Detection

Welcome to the LeafXpert Project, where advanced AI is employed to protect crops from diseases, ensuring the sustainability of our food production and maximizing agricultural yield.

Advanced Image Model

LeafXpert is powered by an advanced AI-based image classification model designed to accurately detect and identify plant diseases from leaf images. The system uses deep learning techniques trained on a diverse dataset covering multiple crops and disease categories, enabling it to recognize subtle visual patterns such as discoloration, spots, texture changes, and deformities. When a user uploads a leaf image, the model processes it in real time, extracts important visual features, and predicts the specific disease with high confidence. The system then provides detailed treatment recommendations and preventive measures to minimize crop damage. Built for reliability and speed, the advanced image model ensures early disease detection, reduces crop loss, and supports smart, data-driven agricultural practices.

Audio Support

LeafXpert provides intelligent audio-based interaction that allows users to communicate with the system using their voice. Farmers and users can ask plant disease–related questions in Tamil, Hindi, or English, making the platform more accessible and user-friendly, especially for those who are not comfortable typing. The system captures the user’s voice input, converts speech into text using AI-powered speech recognition, analyzes the query along with plant disease data, and generates accurate treatment and management recommendations. Responses can also be delivered in the selected language, ensuring clear and effective communication. This multilingual voice support enhances usability in rural areas, enables faster assistance directly from the field, and promotes inclusive smart agriculture through seamless human–AI interaction.

AI ChatBot

LeafXpert includes an intelligent AI Chatbot Assistant designed to provide instant, interactive support for plant disease queries and agricultural guidance. The chatbot understands user questions in Tamil, Hindi, and English, enabling seamless communication for farmers and agricultural professionals. It analyzes user inputs—whether text or converted speech—and generates accurate, context-aware responses based on plant disease knowledge and treatment recommendations. The AI assistant can explain disease symptoms, suggest preventive measures, recommend treatment solutions, and provide general crop care guidance. With real-time interaction and multilingual support, the chatbot enhances accessibility, improves decision-making, and delivers reliable agricultural assistance anytime, anywhere.
